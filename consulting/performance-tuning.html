Performance Tuning

Your dashboards are slow, your queries time out, and your users are frustrated. Let’s fix that.

Is this you?

  • Your Power BI reports take minutes to load instead of seconds
  • SQL Server queries that used to be fast are now crawling
  • Your ETL jobs are taking longer and longer each month
  • You’ve tried Googling for answers but nothing seems to work
  • Management is breathing down your neck about dashboard performance

What I do

I specialize in finding and fixing the root causes of data performance problems. My approach is methodical and transparent — you’ll understand exactly what’s wrong and how to fix it.

My Assessment Process

Step 1: Discover. I review your environment — your SQL Server configuration, your Power BI datasets, your Azure Data Factory pipelines — and identify the biggest bottlenecks.

Step 2: Diagnose. I dig into the details: slow queries, missing indexes, inefficient DAX measures, problematic pipeline configurations. I document every finding with clear explanations.

Step 3: Deliver. You get a prioritized list of fixes, each with estimated impact and effort. Then we work together to implement the changes that matter most.

What You Get

  • Root cause analysis of your performance problems
  • Prioritized recommendations with impact/effort ratings
  • Hands-on implementation of critical fixes
  • Documentation so your team can maintain improvements
  • Follow-up consultation to ensure everything stays fast
