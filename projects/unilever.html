FMCG

Unilever — Pricing & Supply Chain Analytics

Building the data foundation for pricing intelligence and supply chain visibility across FMCG operations.

The Challenge

Unilever’s pricing and supply chain teams were making decisions based on fragmented data. Pricing data came from multiple systems, promotion effectiveness was measured manually, and distribution visibility was limited to periodic reports. The teams needed a unified data model that connected pricing, promotions, and distribution data — and dashboards that delivered real-time insights.

My Role

BI Consultant (Freelance) — designing and delivering the data modelling foundation for pricing intelligence and supply chain visibility.

Architecture

  • Data Modelling: Designed a dimensional model connecting pricing, promotions, and distribution data — enabling cross-functional analytics that hadn’t been possible when the data lived in separate systems.
  • Pricing Analytics: Built DAX measures for price point analysis, promotion effectiveness, and margin tracking, enabling the pricing team to make data-driven decisions.
  • Supply Chain Visibility: Integrated distribution and inventory data to create real-time visibility into product availability and logistics performance.
  • Power BI Dashboards: Delivered interactive dashboards for pricing managers and supply chain leads, with drill-through capabilities and scheduled refresh.

Results

  • Enabled pricing and supply chain teams to make data-driven decisions with real-time distribution visibility
  • Unified pricing, promotions, and distribution data into a single analytics model
  • Reduced manual pricing analysis effort by automating key calculations in DAX
  • Improved promotion effectiveness measurement through connected data

Tech Stack

  • SQL Server
  • Power BI (Desktop & Service)
  • DAX & Power Query (M)
  • Data Modelling (Star Schema)
  • Pricing Analytics
  • Supply Chain Data
