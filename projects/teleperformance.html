Teleperformance — SSOT Reporting Platform

Building the single source of truth for CX operations — consolidating data across platforms into reliable operational dashboards.

The Challenge

Teleperformance’s CX data was scattered across multiple platforms — CRM systems, call center databases, quality assurance tools, and workforce management solutions. Each department produced its own reports, and the numbers rarely matched. Management couldn’t trust the data, and teams spent hours reconciling conflicting figures before every review meeting.

My Role

Senior BI Developer & Azure Data Engineer — responsible for building the SSOT reporting platform, leading ETL development, and mentoring junior data engineers on the team.

Architecture

  • SSOT Data Model: Designed a centralized dimensional model that unified CX metrics across all source systems, ensuring every dashboard pulled from the same validated data.
  • Azure Data Factory Pipelines: Built orchestrated pipelines that ingested data from multiple CX platforms with proper scheduling, dependency chains, and alerting on failures.
  • Azure SQL & Data Storage: Implemented the data layer on Azure SQL with proper indexing, partitioning, and incremental loading to handle large volumes of transactional CX data.
  • Power BI Dashboards: Delivered operational dashboards that management relied on daily — real-time CX KPIs, agent performance, quality scores, and trend analysis.
  • Team Mentorship: Mentored junior data engineers on ETL best practices, DAX patterns, and pipeline reliability standards.

Results

  • Established single source of truth reporting across all CX operations
  • Eliminated data conflicts between departmental reports
  • Reduced reporting reconciliation time from hours to zero
  • Mentored junior data engineers who went on to manage pipelines independently
