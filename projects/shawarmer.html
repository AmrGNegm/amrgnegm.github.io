Shawarmer — Multi-Source BI Platform

Integrating POS, CRM, inventory, and supply chain data into one self-service BI platform for Saudi Arabia’s leading quick-service restaurant.

The Challenge

Shawarmer’s data was fragmented across point-of-sale systems, CRM platforms, inventory management, and supply chain databases. Each department had its own Excel reports, but no one had a complete, consistent view. Management needed real-time visibility into sales performance, supply chain efficiency, and customer behavior — but the data lived in silos.

My Role

BI Developer & Data Engineer (Consulting) — designing and delivering the end-to-end BI platform, from multi-source integration through to executive self-service dashboards.

Architecture

  • Multi-Source Integration: Connected POS, CRM, inventory management, and supply chain databases through ADF pipelines with proper scheduling and dependency management.
  • Data Modelling: Designed a dimensional model for quick-service restaurant analytics — sales by location, product performance, inventory turnover, and customer segmentation.
  • Self-Service Power BI: Built interactive dashboards for operations, sales management, and executive leadership, replacing manual Excel workflows with governed, auto-refreshing reports.
  • Data Quality & Governance: Implemented data validation rules at each pipeline stage to catch discrepancies before they reached the dashboards.

Results

  • Unified 4+ operational data sources into one reporting platform
  • Eliminated manual Excel workflows with automated Power BI dashboards
  • Enabled real-time visibility into sales, inventory, and customer data
  • Reduced report generation time from days to minutes
